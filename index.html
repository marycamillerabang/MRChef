    Welcome to   Mr. Chef Restaurant

Found in the idyllic city of San Jose, Chef Jason “Mr.Chef” Rabang’s eponymous restaurant strikes a perfect balance between its elegant surroundings and a homey ambience. The colonial-style mansion makes for an ideal fine dining destination, with classic Machuca tiles punctuating its well-appointed interiors and lending the restaurant an old-world feel.
